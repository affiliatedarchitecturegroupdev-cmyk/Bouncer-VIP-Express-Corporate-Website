We are not a generalist security company that also covers nightlife. Bouncer VIP Express (Pty) Ltd was founded with a single focus: providing professional, compliant, branded security services to the South African nightlife and entertainment industry.

Why Bouncer VIP Express

The professional
difference

There is a meaningful difference between a registered bouncer on an SLA and a freelancer arranged over WhatsApp. Your venue's licence deserves the former.

The Stakes

Your licence is your
business.

A single SAPS compliance inspection, a patron liability claim or a liquor board inquiry can result in the suspension or revocation of your liquor licence. In South Africa's nightlife industry, unregistered security staff is one of the most common triggers for enforcement action.

Bouncer VIP Express eliminates that exposure entirely. Every bouncer we deploy is PSIRA registered. Every shift is documented. Every engagement is governed by a formal contract. We carry the compliance burden — so you can carry on trading.
